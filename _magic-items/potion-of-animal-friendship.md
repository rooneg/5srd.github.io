---
title: potion of animal friendship
source: |
  [System Reference Document 5.0](https://dnd.wizards.com/articles/features/systems-reference-document-srd)
category: Potion
rarity: uncommon
---

When you drink this potion, you can cast the [*animal friendship*](/spells/animal-friendship/) spell (save DC 13) for 1 hour at will. Agitating this muddy liquid brings little bits into view: a fish scale, a hummingbird tongue, a cat claw, or a squirrel hair.
