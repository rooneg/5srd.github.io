While holding this shield, you have a bonus to AC determined by the shield's rarity. This bonus is in addition to the shield's normal bonus to AC.

| Bonus | Rarity    |
|:-----:|:----------|
|  +1   | Uncommon  |
|  +2   | Rare      |
|  +3   | Very rare |
